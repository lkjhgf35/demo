Our Story

About Riverstone

Our Mission

To know Jesus and make Him known.

We're a non-denominational church rooted in the belief that the Gospel is good news for everyone — regardless of your background, your story, or where you're starting from. Our mission is simple: lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ and help them discover their God-given purpose.

We value authentic community, Spirit-led worship, and practical biblical teaching. We're not a perfect church — but we're a church that believes in a perfect Savior.

Our Values

What drives everything we do

01

Scripture First

The Bible is our compass. Every decision, every teaching, every program begins with "what does God's Word say?"

02

Genuine Community

We don't do life alone. Real relationships, real vulnerability, real support — that's what Riverstone is about.

03

Generous Spirit

We give our time, resources, and love freely — because everything we have is a gift from God.

04

Local Impact

Cedar Falls is our home. We invest deeply in our city — its schools, families, and neighborhoods need the Church.

Pastor Marcus Hale

Lead Pastor

Marcus has led Riverstone since 2012. A Cedar Falls native, he holds a Master of Divinity from Trinity Evangelical Divinity School and is passionate about making the Bible accessible to everyday people. He and his wife Leah have three kids and a rescue dog named Biscuit.
