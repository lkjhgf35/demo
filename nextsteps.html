Getting Connected

Next Steps

Wherever you are on your journey, here's how to go deeper.

Step 01

Plan Your Visit

Tell us you're coming. We'll have a welcome team ready to meet you, show you around, and answer any questions over coffee.

Step 02

Attend Riverstone 101

A casual 2-hour class where you'll learn about our church, our beliefs, and how to get plugged in. Lunch is on us.

Step 03

Join a Small Group

Real life happens in circles, not rows. We have 80+ groups organized by age, life stage, and interest.

Step 04

Serve on a Team

Discover your gifts by serving others. Teams range from hospitality and kids to tech, music, and community outreach.
